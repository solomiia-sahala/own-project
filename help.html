How do I reset or change my password?

If you want to change your password for logging in on your MyourWay account, go to the "My profile" and click on the "Change password" button.

How can I change profile avatar?

If you want to change your avatar on your MyourWay account, go to the "My profile" and click on the "Upload picture" button.

How to add travel story?

Go to the main page and click on the button "Create article", fill all input, upload pictures and "Submit" your story!

Can I upload more that 1 picture to my travel story?

Yes, you can upload more that 1 picture. Choose a few pictures in the "Upload image" section during creating your article.

Can I add markers on the Google map for places I have been visited?

Yes, for adding markers on Google map just right click on place you want to mark.

How to add comment below the other user`s atricle

Below the article on the "Read more" page you can find section "Add comment". Just fill input and "Save" your comment.

How to change my personal info?

Go to "My profile" on naviagation drop-down menu and click on the icon . Change or add your personal info and "Save" it.

How can I navigate between pages?

After logging on the main page, hover the mouse on your name on the right top corner of the page, navigation drop down menu will be displayed.

How to log out from my account?

On the navigation drow-down menu find button "Log out". Click on it and you will logged out.

Can I read more about user throw his travel article?

Yes, click on the user`s nickname under article he added. You will be redirected on the user profile.
